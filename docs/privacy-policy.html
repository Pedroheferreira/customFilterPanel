01

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Custom Filter Panel Power BI visual ("the Visual"), published by Pedro Silva ("we", "us", or "our"), handles information when installed and used within Microsoft Power BI reports and dashboards.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This document explains our data practices in clear and simple terms.

02

Data Collection

The Custom Filter Panel does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information of any kind.

The Visual operates entirely within the local Power BI environment. It reads only the data fields that you explicitly bind to it inside your report, and uses that data solely to render the filter interface on screen. No data leaves your environment.

03

External Connections

The Custom Filter Panel makes no network requests to external servers, APIs, or third-party services. All processing is performed locally within the Power BI rendering engine on the user's device or tenant.

No telemetry, analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics are sent to any server controlled by us or any third party.

04

Data Storage

The Visual does not persist any data beyond the current session. No cookies, local storage, or caches are created. When the Power BI report is closed, no data related to the Visual remains on the user's device.

05

Third-Party Services

The Custom Filter Panel does not integrate with, depend on, or share data with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics providers.

06

Microsoft Power BI Platform

The Visual is distributed through Microsoft AppSource and operates within the Microsoft Power BI platform. Your use of Power BI is governed by Microsoft's Privacy Statement. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for Microsoft's data practices.

07

Children's Privacy

The Custom Filter Panel is a professional business intelligence tool not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

08

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
